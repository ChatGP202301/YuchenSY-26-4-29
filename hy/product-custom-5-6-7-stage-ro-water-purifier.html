PP melt-blown sediment filters use thermally-bonded polypropylene fibers to remove particulate contaminants (rust, sand, silt) from 0.5 to 50 microns. GAC granular activated carbon filters use loose carbon granules for higher contact-time chlorine adsorption and are often the second stage in 5-stage RO systems.
