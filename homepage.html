CARBON BLOCK FILTER

Express Water Carbon Block Filter is NSF certified a high-performance water filter that efficiently removes contaminants from tap water, making it safe and healthy to drink. This filter uses activated carbon technology, which helps to eliminate impurities, such as chlorine, volatile organic compounds (VOCs), and other harmful chemicals.

With its advanced filtration system, Express Water Carbon Block Filter can effectively remove bad taste and odor, while retaining the beneficial minerals present in water. It's easy to install and comes in various sizes to fit different water filtration systems.

GAC Filter Flat Cap

Our Universal GAC filter with Flat Cap Connection is a high-quality solution for water purification. Made with a silicon rubber Flat Cap, this filter is compatible with a range of different water purifiers and is easily installed using a universal connection. The outer shell of the filter can be customized to suit your preferences, with inline and other types available. The GAC filter is designed to effectively remove chlorine, sediment, and other contaminants from your water, ensuring clean and healthy drinking water. With a compact and efficient design, our GAC filter is an ideal choice for improving the quality of your drinking water.
