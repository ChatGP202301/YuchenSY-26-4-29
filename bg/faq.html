Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our standard MOQ starts from 1,000 pieces for OEM/ODM orders. For ready-stock products, we accept smaller quantities. Please contact us via WhatsApp for specific quotes.

❓ What certifications do you have?

We hold NSF/ANSI 42, 53 & 58 certifications, ISO 9001:2015, CE marking, SGS testing reports, FDA registration, and Halal certification. All certificates are available upon request.

❓ What is the production lead time?

Standard products typically ship within 15-20 days. Custom OEM/ODM orders require 25-35 days. Express production is available for urgent orders.

❓ Do you offer OEM/ODM services?

Yes, we provide full OEM/ODM services including custom branding, packaging, product specifications, and exclusive designs for your market.

❓ What payment terms do you accept?

We accept T/T (bank transfer), L/C (letter of credit), Western Union, and PayPal for sample orders. Standard terms: 30% deposit, 70% before shipment.

❓ Can you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to 50+ countries worldwide. We work with major shipping lines and offer FOB, CIF, and DDP terms based on your requirements.
