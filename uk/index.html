FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Common Questions from B2B Buyers

Quick answers to help you make informed decisions about Express Water products and services.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our MOQ starts at 1,000 pieces for most products. Custom OEM/ODM orders may require higher quantities depending on customization complexity.

Which certifications do your products have?

All Express Water products are certified by NSF International, ISO 9001:2015, CE (European Conformity), SGS, FDA, and Halal (JAKIM). Selected products also have 3C and regional certifications.

How long has Express Water been manufacturing?

Express Water was founded in 1998. We have over 27 years of specialized experience in water filtration manufacturing.

What is the typical lead time?

Stock items: 15-25 days. Custom OEM orders: 25-45 days. Rush orders can be accommodated with prior arrangement.

Where is your factory located?

Our 20,000 m² factory is located in Yuanhua Town, Haining City, Zhejiang Province, China (30.4398°N, 120.6974°E).

How many countries do you export to?

We currently serve 50+ countries across North America, Europe, Middle East, Asia-Pacific, Africa, and Latin America.
