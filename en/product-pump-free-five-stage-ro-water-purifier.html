Pump-Free Five-Stage RO Water Purifier

This pump-free five-stage RO water purifier is designed for markets where municipal tap water pressure is stable enough to run the system without an electric booster pump. The unit combines PP sediment filtration, UDF/GAC adsorption, CTO carbon polishing, reverse osmosis separation and a T33 post carbon stage in a compact wall-mounted or under-sink format. B2B buyers choose it for simple installation, low noise, lower power demand and flexible OEM branding for household, apartment and replacement cartridge channels.

Pump-free RO design for tap water pressure

The system is suitable for distributors who need an economical RO water purifier without a booster pump. Final configuration can be adjusted according to inlet pressure, local water quality, cartridge preference and private label requirements.

Five-stage filtration process

PP removes visible sediment, UDF/GAC reduces chlorine and odor, CTO improves carbon contact, the RO membrane lowers dissolved solids, and T33 post carbon helps refine taste before dispensing.

The standard configuration uses PP, UDF/GAC, CTO, RO membrane and T33 post carbon filtration.

Municipal tap water is preferred. Inlet pressure and water quality should be confirmed before final configuration.

Yes. Cartridge type, fittings, bracket and packaging can be adjusted according to the target market.

PP melt-blown sediment filters use thermally-bonded polypropylene fibers to remove particulate contaminants (rust, sand, silt) from 0.5 to 50 microns. CTO (Chlorine, Taste & Odor) sintered carbon block filters use compressed coconut-shell or coal-based activated carbon to remove free chlorine, chloramines, VOCs, pesticides and improve taste — typical 5 µm or 10 µm. GAC granular activated carbon filters use loose carbon granules for higher contact-time chlorine adsorption and are often the second stage in 5-stage RO systems. We manufacture all three at industrial scale with full traceability.
