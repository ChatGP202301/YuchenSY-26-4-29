GAC UDF Filter Cartridge Manufacturer

GAC and UDF activated carbon filter cartridges for RO systems, dispensers and water purifiers. OEM media, shell, label and packaging support.

Request OEM QuoteContact Engineer

Product Introduction

GAC and UDF filter cartridges use granular activated carbon to improve water taste and reduce chlorine and odor in multi-stage drinking water systems. They are a flexible choice for pre-carbon and polishing stages in RO purifiers, dispensers and countertop devices.

Applications

Distributors use GAC/UDF cartridges in replacement filter sets, direct drinking systems, water dispenser filter modules and commercial prefiltration. The granular bed can be configured by fill weight, carbon iodine value, shell design and end pad layout to suit flow and service-life targets.
