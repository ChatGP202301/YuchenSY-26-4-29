E600-S Commercial RO Water Dispenser

Commercial Direct-Drinking Water Dispenser

The E600-S is a vertical RO water dispenser designed for offices, campuses, factories, hotels, cafes and other shared drinking-water areas. Buyers can select 100G or 400G reverse-osmosis purification and a 5 L or 10 L hot tank. Its TDS display includes water-temperature information and a filter life reminder.

Branding note: KAMAMUTA shown in the product photography is sample branding. Private-label badge, panel, manual and carton customization are available for OEM projects.

Display, safety and daily operation

  • LED display shows TDS, water temperature and remaining filter life.
  • Four selectable temperature settings support different workplace drinking needs.
  • One-touch dispensing with separate hot and ambient-water outlets.
  • Intelligent child lock protects the hot-water control.
  • Automatic sleep after three hours without use helps reduce standby energy consumption.
  • Cooling, RO capacity, tank size and pressure-tank arrangement are selected for the actual site.

Configuration must be confirmed before quotation

Send the expected number of users, inlet-water quality, daily water demand, required temperature functions, electrical standard and installation layout. These details determine the appropriate RO capacity, tank arrangement, cooling method and spare-parts package.

Commercial applications

Direct drinking water for shared business environments

This office water dispenser with an integrated RO purifier is suited to reception areas, staff pantries, campuses, factories, hotels, cafes and public-service spaces. Its narrow 400 x 400 mm footprint helps planners position the unit where floor space and access routes are limited.

Project options

100G/400G purification and scalable distribution

The 100G configuration suits moderate point-of-use demand, while 400G provides higher purified-water flow. For multi-point projects, the optional built-in 3.2-gallon pressure tank can support one or two satellite dispensers after pipe length, pressure and simultaneous demand are checked.
