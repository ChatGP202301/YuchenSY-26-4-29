30-inch Three Stage Big Blue Water Filter

Big Blue Water Filter

The 30-inch three stage Big Blue water filter is a high-capacity filtration set for villas, commercial kitchens, light industrial prefiltration and project water treatment. It can combine sediment, carbon and fine filtration stages according to inlet water conditions and buyer requirements. The large housing format supports stronger flow than standard small cartridges. OEM buyers can specify cartridge sequence, housing color, bracket, pressure gauge, inlet/outlet size, logo label, carton design and spare cartridge kits. Main specifications include System Type: Three stage Big Blue filter set; Size: 30 inch housing class; Stages: Sediment, carbon and fine filtration options. Branding details are reviewed after the model is confirmed.
