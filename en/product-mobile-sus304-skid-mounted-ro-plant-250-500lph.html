Mobile SUS304 Skid-Mounted RO Plant 250-500 LPH

Commercial Skid-Mounted RO System

This 250-500 LPH stainless steel RO plant is a compact commercial reverse-osmosis system for buyers who need an accessible vertical layout, SUS304 construction and optional casters. The 500 LPH reference configuration combines dual 20-inch prefilters, two Vontron 4040 membrane elements and panel-mounted monitoring.

Engineering basis: The 500 LPH configuration below is based on a 1000 ppm feed-TDS reference condition. Final output, recovery and membrane selection depend on the actual water analysis, temperature, pressure and operating schedule.

250 or 500 LPH

Select capacity from demand and operating hours

A 500 LPH RO plant is suitable when the project needs about 0.5 m3 of permeate per operating hour under the confirmed design conditions. The 250 LPH version is configured separately; membrane quantity, pump power, controls and electrical load are not assumed from the 500 LPH bill of materials.

  • Confirm hourly and daily treated-water demand
  • Allow for storage-tank volume and peak draw
  • State operating hours and cleaning or sanitizing requirements
  • Confirm available feed flow, pressure and electrical supply
Membrane Selection

Vontron 4040 element options

The 500 LPH reference system uses two Vontron 4040 elements. LP21-4040, ULP21-4040 or XLP11-4040 can be selected according to feed salinity, temperature, pressure and target permeate quality. A membrane model is confirmed in the technical offer rather than treated as a universal specification.

No fixed salt rejection, permeate quality or membrane flow is promised without a feed-water analysis and agreed test conditions.

Instrumentation and Control

Operating information in one panel

The skid combines pressure indication, permeate and concentrate flow adjustment, automatic membrane flushing and conductivity/TDS monitoring. Component brands, alarm logic and control-panel language can be confirmed for the destination market.

Commercial Applications

Compact treatment for distributed projects

The mobile stainless steel skid-mounted RO system can support commercial drinking-water preparation, small production facilities, hospitality back-of-house water, laboratories and other projects with compatible feed water. Application suitability is established from the water analysis and downstream water-quality requirement.

Before Quotation

Information the RO engineer needs

  • Feed-water analysis or at least TDS, hardness, iron, manganese and turbidity
  • Water temperature, available feed pressure and feed flow
  • Required permeate quality, hourly capacity and daily operating hours
  • Storage tank, distribution pump and downstream process
  • Voltage, frequency, phase and destination-country electrical requirements
  • Installation dimensions, drain, pipe connections and caster requirement
  • Control language, remote monitoring and alarm requirements
  • Quantity, OEM label, packing method, destination port and required documents
