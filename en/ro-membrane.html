Product Introduction

An RO membrane element is the replaceable separation cartridge inside a reverse osmosis system. It is not the complete under-sink system; it is the membrane component that reduces dissolved solids, salts and heavy metals after proper sediment and carbon prefiltration.

Applications

Yuchen Water supplies residential and light commercial RO membrane elements for system manufacturers, replacement cartridge distributors and private-label water purifier brands. Common applications include under-sink RO systems, countertop RO purifiers and commercial drinking water machines.
