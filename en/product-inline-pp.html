Buyer selection notes

Inline PP Cartridge should be selected by micron rating, cartridge length, diameter, end-cap style and compatible housing. Importers buying PP melt blown filter cartridge programs should also confirm label artwork, individual bagging, carton quantity and replacement-cycle positioning.

Details to confirm before quotation

  • Micron rating, cartridge length, diameter and end-cap style
  • Compatible housing, flow requirement and replacement interval
  • Food-grade material, sealing method and bulk packing
  • Sleeve label, barcode, carton and pallet requirements
