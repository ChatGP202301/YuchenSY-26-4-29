Built-In Pressure Tank RO Water Purifier 100G/200G

RO Water Purifier

The built-in pressure tank RO water purifier 100G/200G is a compact under-sink system for distributors, importers and appliance brands developing private-label drinking water lines. Its cabinet-ready layout combines PP sediment filtration, UDF/GAC activated carbon, CTO carbon block, RO membrane separation and T33 post-carbon polishing with an integrated storage tank for stable outlet pressure. Filtration Process

How the 5 stage RO water filter works

PP sediment filterReduces sand, rust, silt and suspended particles before the water reaches carbon and membrane stages.
UDF/GAC activated carbonUses granular activated carbon to reduce chlorine, odor and unpleasant taste, helping protect the RO membrane.
CTO carbon blockProvides finer carbon filtration for chlorine, taste, odor and small carbon-particle control before membrane separation.
RO membraneThe reverse osmosis membrane is the core separation stage for dissolved solids reduction in the compact RO system.
T33 post-carbonPolishes final water taste after storage and supports a cleaner drinking experience for end-user installations.
