Small and Medium Seawater Desalination RO Equipment

Small and Medium RO Seawater Desalination

Yuchen Water small and medium seawater desalination RO equipment is built for coastal projects, islands, vessels, hotels and B2B contractors that need reliable drinking or process water from seawater. The system treats 10000-45000 ppm raw water salinity, combines pretreatment with reverse osmosis, and delivers 60 L/h-10000 L/h product water with salt rejection of at least 99.7%. Control, pumps, membrane quantity and protection functions can be customized according to water analysis and project requirements.
