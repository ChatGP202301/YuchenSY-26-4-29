RO Water Purifier

This custom 5/6/7 stage RO water purifier is built for B2B distributors, importers and appliance brands that need flexible reverse osmosis systems for local market programs. Buyers can choose 5, 6 or 7 filtration stages, one pressure gauge, two pressure gauges, different plastic brackets, colored brackets or a simple backboard mounting layout. Yuchen Water supports OEM/ODM branding, filter label design, front panel artwork, export cartons and custom specifications, making the product suitable for private-label RO purifier lines and repeat replacement cartridge sales. For importers and water purifier brands, the 5-stage, 6-stage or 7-stage RO system can be matched to household purifier lines, water dispensers, pre-filtration systems and replacement cartridge programs. OEM/ODM details such as private label, custom color, logo printing, filter label design, carton design, pressure gauge count, bracket style, back plate and export packaging are confirmed by order, while production checks cover raw materials, pressure performance, flow rate, random batch inspection and export document support.
