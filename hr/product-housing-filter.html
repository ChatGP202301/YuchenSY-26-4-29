Eco Express Water Housing Filter

Filter Housing

Eco Express Water is proud to offer its 30 inches Three Stages Big Blue Water Filter, designed to provide you with clean and safe drinking water. With its three stages of filtration (PP Filter, RO Membrane, and CTO Carbon Block Filter), this filter removes contaminants from your water. Proizvedeno u našem pogonu od 20.000+ m² s certifikatom ISO 9001:2015 in Yuanhua Town, Haining City, Zhejiang Province, China, ovaj proizvod je dio naše cjelovite linije za filtraciju vode — neovisno testirano prema NSF/ANSI 42 i 53 standardima od strane SGS-a, with CE marking for European import compliance and FDA-grade material declarations on request. OEM/ODM private-label customization is available: brand printing, custom end-cap molding, color matching and packaging. Standard MOQ from 1,000 pcs; bulk wholesale FOB Shanghai/Ningbo with T/T 30/70 or L/C at sight payment terms. Trenutno izvozimo u 50+ zemalja including the United States, Germany, Russia, Saudi Arabia, Malaysia, Indonesia and Brazil, s punim halal certifikatom on file for Muslim markets.
