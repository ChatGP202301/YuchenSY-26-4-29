Imikhiqizo ekhethiwe ingahlinzekwa noma ihlolwe ngokwe-NSF/ANSI 42, 53 no-58. Amadokhumenti e-ISO 9001, CE, SGS ne-Halal ayatholakala uma eceliwe.

I-MOQ yama-cartridges ajwayelekile we-10" PP, CTO kanye ne-GAC iqala ku-1,000 pcs nge-SKU ngayinye yezinto ezikhona, 3,000 pcs yemikhono ephrintwe nge-OEM, kanye ne-5,000 pcs yemikhiqizo ye-ODM eyenziwe ngezifiso ngokuphelele (i-private end-cap, i-custom O-ring, i-branded shrink-wrap). Ama-UF membrane kanye ne-RO 75/100/400 GPD membranes ngokujwayelekile i-MOQ yi-500 pcs. Abasabalalisi abafakwe odongeni i-MOQ ngamayunithi ayi-100. Ama-oda amancane okuzama angaxoxiswana ngawo kwabasabalalisi abasha.

Isikhungo sethu sokukhiqiza sise-Yuanhua Town, Haining City, Zhejiang Province, e-China — cishe imizuzu eyi-90 ngemoto kusuka e-Shanghai Hongqiao Airport (SHA) kanye nase-Hangzhou Xiaoshan Airport (HGH). Isikhungo esingaphezu kuka-20,000 m² sinezindawo zokukhipha i-PP melt-blown ezizinikele, imishini yokucindezela i-carbon block, amagumbi ahlanzekile okuhlanganisa i-UF/RO membrane, kanye nemigqa yokuhlanganisa ye-SMT/dispenser. Samukela ukuvakashela kwabathengi okuhleliwe, ukuhlolwa kwabantu besithathu (SGS, BV, TÜV) kanye nokuhlolwa kweziqu ze-OEM.

I-PP, CTO, GAC kanye ne-T33 cartridges ejwayelekile enemibala ekhona ithunyelwa zingakapheli izinsuku eziyi-7–10. Ama-cartridges e-OEM private-label anokuphrinta okwenziwe ngezifiso: izinsuku eziyi-20–25 kusukela ekugunyazweni kwemidwebo. Izinto ze-RO/UF membrane: izinsuku eziyi-15–20. Abasabalalisi abafakwe odongeni nabasesitayeleni sedeskithophu: izinsuku eziyi-30–45 kuye ngokwezifiso. Ukulayishwa kwesitsha ngokujwayelekile yi-1 × 20'GP (≈ 80,000 cartridges) noma i-1 × 40'HQ (≈ 200 dispensers). Imigomo ye-FOB Shanghai noma i-Ningbo; CIF, CFR kanye ne-DDP iyatholakala.

Yebo. Sinikezela ngama-reverse osmosis membranes agcwele: 50 GPD, 75 GPD, 100 GPD, 200 GPD, 400 GPD, 600 GPD wezinhlelo zezindlu ezingaphansi kukasinki, kanye ne-300 GPD, 400 GPD kanye ne-600 GPD high-rejection elements yabasabalalisi bamanzi bezentengiselwano abancane. Izinto zezimboni ze-4040 ne-8040 (BW30, SW30 equivalents) ziyatholakala ezitshalweni zokuhlanza amanzi, i-dialysis, i-semiconductor rinse kanye nezicelo zezemithi. Izinga lokulahlwa kukasawoti ≥ 96–99%.

Izihlungi ze-PP melt-blown sediment zisebenzisa ama-polypopylene fibers aboshwe ngokushisa ukuze kukhishwe ukungcola kwezinhlayiyana (ukugqwala, isihlabathi, udaka) kusuka ku-0.5 kuye ku-50 microns. Izihlungi ze-CTO (Chlorine, Taste & Odor) sintered carbon block zisebenzisa i-coconut-shell ecindezelwe noma i-coal-based activated carbon ukuze kukhishwe i-chlorine yamahhala, ama-chloramines, ama-VOCs, imithi yokubulala izinambuzane kanye nokwenza ngcono ukunambitheka — ngokujwayelekile i-5 µm noma i-10 µm. Izihlungi ze-GAC granular activated carbon zisebenzisa ama-carbon granules axekethile okumuncwa kwe-chlorine isikhathi eside sokuxhumana futhi ngokuvamile ziyisigaba sesibili ezinhlelweni ze-RO zezigaba ezi-5. Sikhiqiza zontathu ngezinga lezimboni ngokulandeleka okugcwele.
